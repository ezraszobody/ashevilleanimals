A simple guide to tanks

A tank is a fighting vehicle that started in 1915 and is still used today. They are much more effective than other vehicles because of their ability to drive on rough terrain. They also are more efficient because of their heavy armor; it protects their crew very well. These tanks have very large guns mounted on the head that can often penetrate an enemy tank's armor. These vehicles can range from the "Little Willie" to the M1 Abrams. This website is to give you some information about these amazing vehicles.
